# QuantumAIMobile Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

QuantumAIMobile is a crypto market workspace that includes market monitoring, strategy simulation, wallet activation guidance, and app-level operational tooling.

## Data handling summary

- QuantumAIMobile does not sell personal data.
- QuantumAIMobile does not use third-party advertising SDKs.
- QuantumAIMobile does not use third-party analytics SDKs.
- QuantumAIMobile stores app state, strategy settings, wallet activation progress, and audit history locally on the device.

## Data processed by the app

The app may process the following categories of app data as part of normal operation:

- selected market symbols and watchlist state
- strategy parameters and simulation settings
- locally generated wallet metadata needed for app features
- operational logs and audit records created inside the app
- market data retrieved from external services when live data features are enabled

## How data is used

The data above is used only to:

- operate core app features
- persist user progress and settings on device
- display market and wallet workflows
- improve reliability of in-app synchronization and debugging for the app owner

## External services

When enabled by the user, QuantumAIMobile may request data from external services such as Binance, CoinMarketCap, blockchain RPC endpoints, and wallet deep links. These requests are used to deliver app functionality in real time.

## Data sharing

QuantumAIMobile does not share personal data with data brokers.

## Data retention

Operational state is primarily retained on device. App records may be cleared when the app is removed or local data is reset.
